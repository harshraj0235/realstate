How to Use the Funny Name Generator

Follow these simple steps to generate your own funny names:

  1. Enter a keyword in the input box above. This keyword will be used as the base for generating funny names.
  2. Click the "Generate" button. The generator will create 50 unique funny names based on your keyword.
  3. Browse through the generated names. If you find a name you like, click the "Yes" button to show your appreciation.
  4. If you don't like a name, click the "No" button to generate a new set of funny names.
  5. Have fun and share the funniest names with your friends!
